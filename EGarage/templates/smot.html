Tyre

There are several reasons why you may need replacement tyres, one being that they have worn down. When the tread depth is below the 1.6mm indicator, this can result in skidding. If you are involved in a road collision and your tyres are found to be below the legal limit, you could be dealt a fine or lose your licence. You may also need a new tyres if you have a slow puncture, this can easily happen when you drive over sharp objects.
