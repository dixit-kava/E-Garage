2.Servicing

A service is not legal requierment like MOT, but they are still recommended in between MOTs. A service is a vehicle inspection based on guidelines provided by vehicle manufacturer. There are two types of service; an interim service that should be carried out evry 6 months and a full service that is recommended evry 12 months.
